Phone calls, memory, and manual follow-up. Every business leaks leads the same way. Here's exactly where.

Every call after 5pm, on weekends, or while you're on-site is a lead that will call your competitor next. An AI voice receptionist answers every time.

02 / Lost leads

Leads falling through the cracks

Inquiries pile up across texts, DMs, and email. With no system, some get forgotten. That's not a discipline problem — it's a missing CRM problem.

Contacts living in your head

You can't manage what you can't see. Without a pipeline, you have no idea which leads are hot, cold, or silently deciding to go with someone else.

04 / No follow-up

Follow-up that never happens

You know you should follow up. But when you're delivering the job, it falls off. Automated sequences fix this — they run whether you remember or not.
