The problem

Every missed call is a missed job.

Vancouver service businesses lose thousands in revenue every month to unanswered calls. When you're on a job, with a client, or asleep — your phone rings and nobody picks up. That caller moves on to your competitor.

Calls going to voicemail after hours

Over 60% of callers won't leave a voicemail. They call the next business on Google. If you can't answer after 5pm or on weekends, those leads are gone before you even know they called.

No-shows and unbooked appointments

When leads have to wait for a callback to book, they forget, get cold, or find someone who booked them instantly. Friction in your booking process costs real revenue every week.

You can't answer and work at the same time

You're in the middle of a job. Your phone rings. You ignore it because you have to. That's the moment you lost a $3,000 contract. A voice AI handles it while you keep working.

The solution

How it works

Your AI receptionist runs in the background — silent when you don't need it, active the moment a call comes in.

1

Call comes in — AI answers instantly

The moment your phone rings, the AI picks up within one to two seconds. No hold music. No voicemail. A natural, professional voice greets the caller immediately, any time of day.

2

Greets the caller in your business name

The AI speaks with knowledge of your services, your pricing, and your business. It introduces itself as part of your team — not as a robot. Most callers can't tell the difference.

3

Qualifies, books, or transfers urgent calls

The AI qualifies the caller, books appointments directly into your calendar, and answers common questions. If the call is urgent, it transfers the caller to you live — right then, right there.

4

You get a summary by text or email

After every call, you receive a concise summary — who called, what they wanted, what the AI said, and whether an appointment was booked. You stay informed without picking up the phone.
